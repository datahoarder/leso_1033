 Welcome to The Directory

In addition to the customer contact center information below and numbers for public or news media inquiries, the directory lists phone numbers for each major DLA Disposition Services function or program.

Customer Help 

Contact the DLA Customer Interaction Center (CIC) at 1-877-DLA-CALL (1-877-352-2255) or email them at DLAContactCenter@dla.mil, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Media and Public Inquiries 

Media or the public with questions about DLA Disposition Services can call 269-961-7015 during normal weekday business hours. Media with urgent afterhours or emergency information needs should call 703-767-6200.
